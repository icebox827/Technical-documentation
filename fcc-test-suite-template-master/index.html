Engine

The heart and soul of your vehicle is the internal combustion engine.

The engine is made up of the following parts (among others):

  • Timing chain
  • Camshaft
  • Crankshaft

The enginePower() method calculates engine under different conditions.

Electrical System

The electrical system starts and keeps all your cars components running.

The electrical system is made up of the following parts:

  • Battery
  • Alternator
  • Fuse

The electricalFlow() method estimates efficiency of electrical flow.

Cooling System

The cooling system is responsible for helping the engine keep cool.

The radiator is compose

  • Radiator
  • Fan
  • Hoses

The coolingEfficiency() method estimates the efficiency of your cooling system.

Braking System

Found on all four wheels, your brakes are one of the most important safety systems on your vehicle.

The braking system is made op of the following parts:

  • Brake pedal
  • Brake discs
  • Brake pads

The brakingSecurity method estimates the responsiveness of your braking system.

Steering - Suspension

The steering and suspension helps improve the ride and handling of the vehicle.

The steering and suspension is made up of the following parts:

  • Steering wheel
  • Shocks/struts
  • Ball joints

The steeringSuspensionEase method estimates the smoothness of your steering and suspension.
